Our Mission

Theater education without barriers

Every student deserves to learn the craft of theater — not just performance, but the technical artistry behind the curtain. Stage design, lighting, and set construction are often invisible disciplines, taught only where resources allow.

Upstage is a non-profit web application giving any school or community theater access to professional-grade design tools, guided lessons, and a collaborative environment that mirrors real-world theater workflows.
